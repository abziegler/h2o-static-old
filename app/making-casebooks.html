Get Started

H2O helps law faculty make digital casebooks that are free, simple to update and easy to access and print.

All of the content on H2O is free to access and covered by a CC BY-NC-SA 3.0 license.

Sign Up

To make casebooks you'll need an account. Sign up for free here.

Once you login, your account menu will appear in the upper right corner, and from there you can access your profile and dashboard or sign out.

Complete Your Profile

Complete your profile by adding your display name and your school. Request verified professor status to get a green check mark and ensure that your casebooks are included in search results.

Dashboard

Your dashboard is where you'll find all the casebooks you've authored. Once you've logged in, access your dashboard from the account menu in the upper right corner of the screen.

Click on any casebook to visit it. Published casebooks are marked with a blue border and a "Published" label. Draft casebooks are marked with a yellow border and a "Draft" label.

Published casebooks with revisions-in-process have a yellow banner that says "This casebook has unpublished changes." To learn more about revising casebooks, visit the revising casebooks section of this guide,
